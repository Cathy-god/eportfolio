Welcome

This ePortfolio documents my critical reflections, personal insights, and team experiences throughout the intensive program on Social Entrepreneurship, Innovation & Design.

Week 1: Foundations & Team Decisions

Key Components of Week One: Explored fundamental concepts such as Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), design thinking, problem framing, and hypothesis testing. Applied models like the Problem Tree Analysis and Lean Startup methodology.

Team Decision-Making: Our team selected a problem through structured brainstorming and multi-voting. My contribution involved researching food insecurity and presenting data on its social impact.

New Learnings & Reflections: Gained insights into how defining a clear problem statement influences the success of a solution. Learned the importance of empathy mapping when designing user-centered solutions.

Week 2: Overcoming Challenges & Innovation

Challenges Faced: Navigated resource limitations, conflicting stakeholder needs, and adapting to changing feedback. Example: Adjusted our prototype after mentor consultations.

Innovation & Prototyping: Developed a digital mock-up for a mobile app connecting surplus food to food-insecure students. My role included user research and designing key platform features.

Team Pitch & Reflections: Learned how to structure a compelling pitch and tailor it to different audiences. Key achievements included positive feedback from mentors and a refined prototype. Areas for improvement involved better time management in the prototyping phase.

Final Reflection: Key Takeaways & Learnings

Problem Identification & Innovation: Developed the ability to recognize systemic challenges and propose scalable solutions.

Overall Experience: The most personally meaningful aspect was refining a social enterprise idea with real-world potential. The biggest challenge was working asynchronously due to personal constraints.

Collaboration & Communication: Developed teamwork skills using virtual platforms like Miro and Slack.

Future Applications: Plan to apply these insights in entrepreneurial projects and further studies in social impact innovation.
